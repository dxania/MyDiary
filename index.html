About

MyDiary is an online based diary where you can pen down your thoughts, mark your appointments, document your memories. It also comes with special reminder and notification features that will prompt you to add entries to your diary.
With this application, you do not need to worry about the loss of your journal or having to move around with a pen and diary, MyDiary is available 24/7 and you can get access to your content from anywhere, at anytime and at your leisure.
